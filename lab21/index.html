Lab 21: 2nd Draft Team Project Sketches

Team Feedback

  1. What elements help to present a clear and interesting web page?
    • The left 'block' navbar looks neat and could easily be standardized to match other group members' pages
    • The portrait and biography together at the top help solidify the topic and seeing George's face makes his poetry feel more personal
    • Putting the poetry next to the commentary and under the portrait & bio breaks up the clutter and allows for better comparison
  2. What would you add to the design and why?
    • More images of Carmel, SF, and George along with other audio and visual additions would help define George's voice and connect him to the reader
    • Tabs for each poem or piece of major information placed at the top of the page could reduce clutter and clarify the page's sections
    • Slideshows/dropdowns for photos and text could also clean up the page
  3. What would you change or remove from the design and why?
    • Most sketches have too much going on, some elements could be organized (like with tabs), but some such as the multiple asides can be consolidated
    • The text elements like poems, bio, and commentary should include images to liven up the page, and be connected to their relevant elements
    • The nav bar and footer should have a format and layout that can be used by all group members

Challenge

This lab actually ended up being less challenging for me than lab 20, I could put more detail and time into my sketches while still remembering that they didn't have to be perfect. What was difficult was incorporating my class and teammates feedback such as reducing clutter and the addition of certain elements.

Problems

I didn't run into any problems doing this lab, I made the webpage using bootstrap and other things that we've learned and made my sketches following the previous and new guidelines and examples. If anything was a problem I'd say it was having too many ideas!

Results

On completion of this lab I now have three great sketches that I can share with my team and use as a basis for building my webpage and to inform some of the unified elements such as the nav bar on all our team member's pages. This lab has gotten me excited to make my page and the past few labs have introduced and given me needed practice using several helpful tools for brainstorming and prototyping.
