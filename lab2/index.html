“Being vulnerable is not about fear, grief, or disappointment, it’s the birthplace of everything we are hungry for.”

I believe the things the human heart is hungry for are basic needs: love, belonging, joy, empathy. Allowing yourself to feel this way in front of others will open your mind for understanding and your heart for comfort. I’m going to share a little bit about myself with you today in hopes that you would share your own vulnerabilities with me. I spend a lot of my free time drawing and writing. These pastimes are easy for me, mostly because I love the distraction. I have an extremely hard time being alone, so when I can sit down with a pencil and paper and get my thoughts or ideas down it makes me feel a little less disconnected. As if my ideas are on paper that someone validates them.
