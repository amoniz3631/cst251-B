Lab 15: Fancy CSS 1

box shadow

Challenge

The most challenging property we had to implement was making a box-shadow. Even after looking at different websites it wasn't quite clear which edge each unit correlated to. The Google inspect tool once again came in handy to figure this out.

Problems

The problem we keep running into is our nav bars not sticking despite having a position of sticky. In order to solve this we tried changing the height of the page, the z-index of elements, and the height and width of the nav bar itself. None of those solutions worked, but a forum on Free Code Camp suggested changing the position to fixed instead which 'fixed' it so to speak.

Results

This lab resulted in us creating a lab 15 page that helped us to learn how to use shadows, gradients, filters, and other new styling properties to enhance the look of our pages.
